77% of Americans now believe in climate change, including 59% of Republicans. In fact, 67% of people support President Obama's plan to regulate power plants to cut carbon emissions. But our elected officials don't quite line up. A majority of Congress consists of Climate Deniers. In fact, in 2014, Politifact claimed that there were only 8 Republicans in Congress who did not deny the existence of climate change. We've been a little more generous in our findings, but it appears that 54% of Congress, and 95% of Republican Congressmen, are denying the fact of climate change.
